What Has Python DONE?

Python has accomplished many great things and has been used by my great people. Below is a list of all of Python's achievements, both as a lanugage and as a greater community.


  • Python has remained in the Top 10 of the TIOBE Index for the most popular turing complete languages since 2004! It even managed to reach the #1 spot in October of 2021.

  • Due to it's ease-of-use, accessibility, and quick run times, Python is as a very popular scripting langauge.

  • With the addition of the SciPy, Matplolib, and NumPy libraries, Python has become highly effective in the field of scientific computing.

  • Python is ranked as the 4th most used programming langauge in the world, ranking only behind web-based and database languages such as: HTML/CSS, JavaScript, and SQL.

  • The impact to Python's success isn't just limited to the scientific community, the entertainment industry uses Python every single day via the Industrial Light & Magic Company. This visual effects powerhouse was created by George Lucas in 1975 in order to create special effects for the original Star Wars film. This company has gone on to win multiple Academy Awards for Best Visual Effects, all thanks to the careful and simplistic control of Python over their production pipelines.

  • In terms of pure success, Frequentis stands atop all other Python achievements. Frequentis is one of the leading providers of safety-critical solutions for Air Traffic Management and Public Safety & Transport. Python itself plays a crucial rule in Frequentis' TAPtools product, which is used globally to track weather conditions, control runway lighting, and monitor/control navigational aid instruments.

To find a complete list of Python's Success Stories, please visit Python.org/about/success/.
